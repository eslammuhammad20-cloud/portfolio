About Me

I am a Pre-Sales & Tender Engineer specialising in Low Voltage switchgear systems, with a proven track record of converting complex technical specifications into commercially optimised, winning proposals for major infrastructure projects across Saudi Arabia and Egypt.

My expertise spans the full Schneider Electric LV portfolio — BlokSet, Prisma P/G, PrismaSeT I, and Disbo — combined with hands-on proficiency in IEC 61439 compliance, short-circuit analysis via Ecodial, and bus bar selection. I also maintain working knowledge of MV distribution systems, transformer sizing, and MV/LV interface design, positioning me for broader power distribution roles.

Beyond technical execution, I take a commercial and client-facing approach — presenting and defending technical submittals, negotiating deviations with procurement and suppliers, and collaborating closely with consultants and end users to accelerate approval cycles and reduce revision loops.

A self-driven technologist, I have built my own suite of AI-assisted and Excel-based engineering tools — including a Claude AI-powered LV panel spec analyser and custom Schneider Electric reference selectors with 2,700+ auto-populating catalog data points — significantly cutting BOM preparation time across multi-million SAR tender workflows.
